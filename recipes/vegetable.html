Lasagna Recipe

Vegetable Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store‑bought or homemade)
  • ½–¾ cup pizza sauce
  • 2–3 cups shredded mozzarella
  • Mixed veggies: bell pepper, red onion, mushrooms, olives, cherry tomatoes
  • 1–2 tbsp olive oil, Italian seasoning, salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C) with a stone or sheet inside. Prep veggies in thin slices.
  2. Stretch dough on parchment; brush lightly with olive oil. Spread sauce evenly.
  3. Top with mozzarella, then arrange vegetables. Season with Italian herbs, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bake 10–14 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbling. Rest 2–3 minutes, slice, and serve.
