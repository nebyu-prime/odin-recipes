Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles (or no‑boil sheets)
  • 1 lb ground beef or Italian sausage, plus 1 small onion and 2 cloves garlic
  • 4 cups tomato sauce (or crushed tomatoes) with Italian seasoning
  • 15 oz ricotta, 1 egg, ½ cup grated Parmesan
  • 3–4 cups shredded mozzarella

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 375°F (190°C). Boil noodles until al dente if not using no‑boil; drain and set aside.
  2. Brown meat with onion in a little oil; add garlic, then tomato sauce and seasoning. Simmer 5–10 minutes.
  3. Mix ricotta with egg and Parmesan; season lightly with salt and pepper.
  4. Layer in a 9×13 pan: thin sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella. Repeat 2–3 times; finish with sauce and mozzarella. Cover and bake 25–30 min, uncover 10–15 min until bubbly. Rest 10 min before slicing.
