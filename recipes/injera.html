Injera Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups teff flour (or 50% teff + 50% all‑purpose)
  • 2½–3 cups warm water
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast or ½ cup sourdough starter
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Neutral oil or ghee for the pan (optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine teff flour and water in a bowl to form a thin pancake‑like batter. Stir in yeast or starter. Cover and ferment 12–24 hours until bubbly and slightly tangy.
  2. Stir in salt and add a splash of water if needed; the batter should pour easily but not be watery.
  3. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium. Lightly oil if needed. Pour batter in a spiral to cover the surface; cook 1–2 minutes until holes form and the top sets. Do not flip.
  4. Transfer to a cloth or plate to cool slightly. Repeat with remaining batter and serve warm.
